Our Journey has Started from 18th Century

Land was Discovered - 1750

People from near villages have found land. Which was unsuitable for cultivation. The land was filled with lot of stones and trees. A forest like environment was Discovered.

Agricultural Revolution - 1770

It took several years to make cultivable land. People started doing various types of agricultural activities and It had become normal.

Small Town was Born - 1780

Few people named their occupied land. Finally, One of the name was popular. That's Kudapattu. The greatest small town was born.

Life has Started - 1800

Some people start to live from their land. Work Life balance has become easy after life moved on thier land. Normal life has been started in small town.

Light has Arraived - 1880

Electricity was come for agricultural land. After few years people started to get electricity for home. Finally, Light has arraived on their life.
